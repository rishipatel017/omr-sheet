How to use OMR Pro

  1. Configure your sheet settings at Top of the page in header section(Name, Questions, Options, Columns)
  2. Click Generate to create your OMR sheet
  3. Mark answers manually or paste student answers
  4. Paste the answer key in the evaluation panel
  5. Click Evaluate to see your results
